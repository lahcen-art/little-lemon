Our Story

Welcome to Little Lemon, a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist. Our chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Greek, and Turkish culture and have a menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally.

Our History

Founded in 2010 by brothers Mario and Adrian, Little Lemon has been serving the community with love and dedication for over a decade. What started as a small family restaurant has grown into a beloved local establishment known for its authentic flavors and warm hospitality.

Our Philosophy

We believe in using only the freshest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible. Our menu changes with the seasons, ensuring that we're always serving dishes made with produce at its peak flavor.
